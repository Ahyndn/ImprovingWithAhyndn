# Chapter 2: Nowhere or Software

## My 2020 Emotional Uplift
During the holidays, I received the most heart-warming holiday message ever and this stopped from possibly wasting a large part of my 2021 being dejected after failing a critical life changing interview. So, this one message made me stop presenting myself as a piece of useless disappointment to the people that mean a lot to me. I wanted to start presenting myself as more respectable, responsible and commendable. Therefore, I took on a rare piece of advice from my interactions with all the people to date, I decided software to be my profession.

## 2 Educational Paths, 1 Final Answer: My Career
I saw that software development had 2 educational paths:

1. Software Professionals that educate software development as technical skills
2. Software Professionals that educate software development as a profession

Ultimately, I am a software developer remains as my final answer. Thus, in the last bit of 2020 and going into 2021, I would spend time with these professionals.
